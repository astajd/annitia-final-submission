## Reading guide

- `oof_weighted` is the score we ultimately care about; `fold_std`/`fold_min` tell you whether the gain is fragile.

- `rank_corr_with_current_state_v2_*` indicates how much new information the variant carries vs the current public best (lower = more diversity, but also more risk of drifting away).
